What is JIL.AI token?

Utilizing blockchain (token) technology & AI-driven Wealth Management Strategies

JIL.AI token is the governance token of the Global Charitable DAO ecosystem, designed to empower the community in making charitable funding decisions through decentralized governance.

We provide financial support to individuals and organizations dedicated to addressing these critical global issues.

Our platform ensures transparency & accountability through decentralized oversight and financial auditing.

With a donor-centric approach, we collaborate with humanitarian entities while maintaining operational independence.

What is our motivation?

Crimes against humanity are pervasive issues that generate an estimated $150 billion annually in illegal profits, making crimes against humanity the second-largest illicit trade after drugs.

These crimes affect every region of the world, with vulnerable populations in developing nations being disproportionately targeted.

According to global estimates, more than 40 million people are trapped in slavery-like conditions, spanning forced labor, sexual exploitation, and coerced servitude.

Despite global condemnation and increased awareness, the gap between financial resources available for combating these crimes and the scale of the problem remains significant.
